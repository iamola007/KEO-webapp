Who We Are


Have folks who can neither read nor write? Let our vocal guide help them carry out simple transactions with options in Hausa, Igbo & Yoruba.


KEO Wallet is an alternative Financial service provide that consists of a low data usage mobile app and web app that is focused on providing a seamless transaction experience to the uneducated and underbanked.


We are on a mission to reducing the digital inequality and digital divide for social and economic development.
