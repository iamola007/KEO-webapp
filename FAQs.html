Questions? Look here

What makes KEO wallet different?

We have a vocal guide which ensures that everyone, educated or not has access to our financial services.

Do you give loans?

No, we do not give loans yet but you’ll be able to get a loan from us in the future if you use your wallet regularly.

What do I need to open a KEO Wallet account?

To open a KEO Wallet you must be 16 years old, have a phone number, a BVN or NIN.

Why do you need my BVN?

We ask for your BVN to confirm your identity, so that we can be sure no one is pretending to be you. When we confirm your BVN, we match your details with the information on the national BVN database owned by The Central Bank of Nigeria.

Is there a minimum balance?

No, we do not have a minimum balance.
